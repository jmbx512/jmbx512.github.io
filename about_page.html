What got me started

I was first introduced to construction and architecture during a trip I took to Singapore. I visited many of the country's defining landmarks, such as The Cloud Forest and Gardens by the Bay, which showcased seamless integration of architectural composition and structural integrity. This experience made me appreciate the balance between form and function when it comes to designing infrasturure.

Since then, I have pursued Building Science, an interdisciplinary area of study that incorporates aspects of both engineering and architecture. Through studio classes, I have learned to bring creativity and technical expertise to my projects with the goal of showcasing beauty and system.

Recently, I have been exploring BIM and 3D modeling. I am fascinated by the use of software to organize entire consturction projects, helping develop the physical world. Moving forward, I hope to delve more into this discipline and contribute to residential projects, facade work, and sustainable design.
