Static Earthquake

USC ARCH 205A Project


Timeline

November 2022 - December 2022


Proposal

Design and construct a full-scale space capturing a phenomenon. Examples of potential phenomena include color, shadow, motion, sound, gravity, and waves. Work in a team to construct a space that explores the phenomena of motion through the replication of a static earthquake. Wood pieces and metal fasteners are source of building materials.
